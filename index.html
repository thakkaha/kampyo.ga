What is kampYO.ga?

kampYO.ga is a family-oriented residential YOga workshop held in Ganaraska Forest Centre (Campbellcroft, ON). It is by and for volunteers of HSS Canada in a friendly, informal and fun-filled atmosphere. Its first edition (in Oct 2016) saw about 40 participants from about 10 families. The numbers doubled in its 2nd edition in May 2017 (About 80 participants from 20 families!). During Oct 2017, 35 again participated despite karva-chauth festivities :-) AND then in May 2018 it further keeps growing. May 2019 was 5th edition. In May 2022, there were two groups 6a & 6b!
Expect to participate in the following YO.ga activities:

  • Classes
  • Conversations
  • Games
