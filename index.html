What is campYO.ga?

campYO.ga is a family-oriented residential YOga workshop held in Ganaraska Forest Centre (Campbellcroft, ON). It is by and for volunteers of HSS Canada in a friendly, informal and fun-filled atmosphere. Its first edition (in Oct 2016) saw about 40 participants from about 10 families. The numbers doubled in its 2nd edition in May 2017 (About 80 participants from 20 families!). During Oct 2017, 35 again participated despite karva-chauth festivities :-) AND then in May 2018 it further keeps growing!
Expect to participate in the following YO.ga activities:

  • Classes
  • Conversations
  • Games
What to bring?

Apart from your enthusiasm & smiling face, also bring:

  • Yoga Mat
  • Personal accessories
  • Necessary travel documents to enter US & return back to Canada
